Blood-brain barrier permeability is an important property in drug discovery.

The blood-brain barrier protects the central nervous system from harmful compounds.

Compounds predicted as BBB permeable may reach the brain and exert neurological effects.

Morgan fingerprints are circular molecular fingerprints widely used in cheminformatics and molecular machine learning.

XGBoost is a gradient boosted decision tree algorithm commonly used for molecular property prediction.

The BBBP dataset is a binary classification dataset used to predict blood-brain barrier permeability from molecular structure.

RDKit is an open-source cheminformatics toolkit used for molecular descriptor calculation and fingerprint generation.
